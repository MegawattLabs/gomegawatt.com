Our process

 

  • Step 1

    Audience

    Campaigns start with target audiences. Our team combines research, third-party data and behavior to ensure the right segments are reached.

  • Step 2

    Strategy

    Based on audience segments, we develop cross-channel strategies that will allow for testing, evaluation, and optimization.

  • Step 3

    Creative

    Your creative needs to meet your customer with the right message on the right device. We make sure you focus on ideas instead of formatting.

  • Step 4

    Sprint

    Once we’ve received creative, it’s time to begin the campaign. You’ll be able to analyze performance in real-time through our analytics platform.

  • Step 5

    Analytics

    The MegaWatt platform provides 100% campaign transparency. Our team interprets the details so you can share successes throughout your company.

  • Rinse
    and
    Repeat!
